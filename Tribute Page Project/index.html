Brendan Eich

The mind behind JavaScript and Rust

This is the biography of Brendan Eich

      ♦ In 1961 Brendan was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania (USA).
      ♦ He studied at Ellwood P. Cubberley High School and graduated in 1979.
      ♦ At the University of Santa Clara, he studied computer science and mathematics. His master's degree was received in 1985 from the University of Illinois.
      ♦ Brendan worked up at Netscape Communications Corporation in 1995 it was there where the magic began to create JavaScript, in that year there was a boom on the internet and the demand that browsers were beginning to generate. Brendan used Scheme (OOP) and some of the JAVA syntax.
      A new language was born and the most difficult thing was choosing the name it would bear, first it was Mocha, then LiveScript, and finally JavaScript because the boom in these moment was JAVA. JavaScript was accepted by the ECMA organization in 1996.
      ♦ In 1998 Eich co-founded Mozilla with Jamie Zawinski, was a free software project, and served as Mozilla's lead architect and engineer.
      ♦ Mozilla appointed Brendan as CEO in 2014 and he only lasted 11 days from March 24 to April 3, this was due to social criticism and how his image was seen regarding equal marriage and his ideological position in this regard. After this he finally resigned from Mozilla.
      ♦ Since 2016 and is currently the CEO of Brave Software, an open source, privacy-focused, Chromium-based web browser development company for developers.
